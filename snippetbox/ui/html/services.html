Our Services

  • Cardiologist

    Spirometry: Examination of external respiratory function to diagnose and monitor the respiratory system.

  • Pediatrician

    Neonatology: Care of newborns, including diagnosis and treatment of problems specific to newborns. General physical examination of children, including assessment of growth, development, and general health. Vaccination: Administering vaccinations to prevent various infectious diseases in children.

  • Otolaryngologist

    Endoscopic examination of ENT organs.

  • Gastroenterologist

    Colonoscopy: Endoscopic examination of the large intestine to detect abnormalities and diseases.

  • Endocrinologist

    Thyroid Ultrasound: Diagnosis of thyroid conditions using ultrasound technology. Hormone tests: Evaluation of hormone levels in the blood to diagnose endocrine disorders.

  • Gynecologist

    Colposcopy: Examination of the cervix using a colposcope to detect pathologies and precancerous conditions. Pelvic Ultrasound: Diagnosis of the female reproductive organs using ultrasound technology.

  • Urologist

    Urinary tract ultrasound: Diagnosis of the condition of the urinary tract organs using ultrasound technology.

  • Proctologist

    Rectoromanoscopy: Examination of the rectum and anus area using a rectoromanoscope to detect abnormalities.
